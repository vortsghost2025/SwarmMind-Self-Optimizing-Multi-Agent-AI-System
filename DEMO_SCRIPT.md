## 🎯 KEY MOMENTS TO EMPHASIZE

1. **The Trace Reveal (2:00-2:45)** - This is your "wow" moment
2. **The Clear Explanation** - Avoid jargon, use analogies everyone understands
3. **The Confident Delivery** - Show pride in what you've built
4. **The Human Connection** - Remember: AI should serve humanity, not replace it

## 🚫 WHAT NOT TO DO

- Don't get lost in technical details
- Don't apologize for simplicity (simplicity is sophisticated)
- Don't mention what you didn't build (focus on what you did)
- Don't read monotonously — tell a story

## ✅ PRO TIPS

- Practice the timing — aim for 3:30 to leave room for questions
- Smile when talking about the cognitive trace — it's genuinely exciting
- Be ready to explain one technical depth if asked, but keep the demo accessible
- Have water nearby — you'll be talking for several minutes straight
